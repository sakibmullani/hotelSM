Panhala Fort:-
	

                        Largest of all the Deccan forts, the Panhala Fort is believed to be built between the year 1178 to 1209 AD. The wall of the fort is of triangular 
                       form and is about seven kilometres long. The 3 gates resulting in the central grounds of the fort have completely different inscriptions on them.

                       The third gate consists of Lord Hanuman and a lotus inscribed thereon whereas the second gate consists of Lord Ganesh alongside 2 lions. the primary gate 
			of the fort consists of a block with inscriptions in Persian of that of Adil Shah.

                       It is known for its natural beauty and a very cool climate . It is a pleasant mini hill station.
